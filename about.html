About TDEE Calculator

TDEE Calculator is a free online health and fitness tool designed to help you calculate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure — the total number of calories your body burns in a day.

Our mission is to make calorie and fitness tracking simple and accessible for everyone. Whether your goal is weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain, our calculator gives you accurate estimates based on scientific formulas like the Mifflin-St Jeor equation.

We believe fitness should be data-driven and easy to understand. That’s why we built this platform — to empower users with accurate tools and clear insights about their daily energy needs.
